Hector Bellerin

My inspiration

----------------------

Growing up, I played a lot of soccer within my years in Highschool. I always idolized Hector, for his talents, his attitude, and his style in clothing.

To me he was the complete player. He was fast, a defense player like me, and he played for my favorite club at that time which was Arsenal FC.

For me that was a main reason for academic and athletic success as I wanted to be in his shoes. Although that didn't workout as I wanted to,

I can still say he was a huge inspiration for me growing up.
